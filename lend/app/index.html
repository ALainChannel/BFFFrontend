FAQ

When will the first cafes of the BITCOFFEEN® network open?

The BITCOFFEEN® as a brand did not plan to carry out an Initial Coin Offering and implement the launch of a cafe network without attracting third- party funds. At the same time, due to the increasing popularity of the BITCOFFEEN® project and the desire of investors to take part in the development of the ecosystem BITCOFFEEN® brand is putting up for sale a limited number of 1,000,000 coins at a price of 90 cents.

When will the BITCOFFEEN® mobile app be available to download?

The BITCOFFEEN® mobile app is one of the key elements of the ecosystem. At present, our specialists are engaged in debugging and testing of the app and the work is expected to be completed in a third quarter of 2019.

Where to buy BFF coins?

At the time of presale which will be held at www.bitcoffeen.com website only 1,000,000 coins will be offered for sale. Coins will be sold at a price of $ 0.9 per 1BFF, and at the end of the resale and the actual launch of the BITCOFFEEN® network, all BFF coins in free circulation will have a nominal value of at least $ 1. In the future, as new outlets open in other cities and countries, the number of BFF coins required for their uninterrupted operation will be increased through subsequent presales, however all the future subsequent sales of the BFF coin cannot be less than the price of $ 1 per coin.

How to become a territorial partner of BITCOFFEEN®?

The territorial partner is an authorized representative of the owner of the BITCOFFEEN® trademark in its region. Partnership requires: - One-off contribution fee of 10,000 BFF coins. - Active participation in the development of the BITCOFFEEN® network in your region. - Signing of a bilateral agreement. If regional operator systematically failing to comply with the terms of the contract, BITCOFFEEN® reserves the right to terminate the contract and transfer the Territorial representation to another person.

I want to open a cafe using the BITCOFFEEN® system, how do I join in ?

BITCOFFEEN® is designed to simplify the process of opening a cafe and as soon as possible to receive the maximum profit from your outlet. The automated BITCOFFEEN® mobile application will greatly simplify your work which minimizes your financial costs to operate a business within the network. All this together will contribute to the flow of customers to your coffee shop. To get started, you just need to install a mobile application and register with the network. Network users can quickly get answers from our online support for franchisees. Self-printing and generation of QR codes for networking allows the franchisee to produce codes on-the-go as when they required.

How does BITCOFFEEN® generates profits to pay an interest to its investors?

BITCOFFEEN® brand makes profits from selling QR codes to franchisees. The BITCOFFEEN® franchisee pays for Royalty, which with the help of Blockchain makes its transparent distribution amongst all participants of the ecosystem.

Why do we need Blockchain?

For the BITCOFFEEN® technology Blockchain is not just a big word but it is a cost effective technology which includes 1. Record keeping system 2. Transparent distribution system 3. Monitoring system 4. Safety system These are 4 key reasons why BITCOFFEEN® uses Blockchain!
